About

Founded in winter of 2020, a group of "stray cats" living off the frigid streets of Chicago, got together with the hopes of finding warm homes. The goal was to find humans to take them in, but they didn't want to lose there feline rights. They wanted a home, but were not willing to become pets, like "dogs." The group came up with a plan to teach cats to train humans to treat them with the respect they deserve while living comfortably in a human residence.

Mission

We are professional cats that will train you to train your humans. We are 100% confident that any feline can become a ProCat!
